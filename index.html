Events

Startinev engages with start-ups by hosting a competition for local entrepreneurs. This often opens the door to collaborating with the winners. This is done through recruitment, incubating, acquisition, etc.
Hackathons are a creative way to crowdsource ideas and solutions for the business. When the corporate identifies a problem and does't have a well defined solution or when there is a major insight on an idea but do not have a full fledged vision for the product, crowdsourcing is a great solution to this. Startinev therefore works with you to activate innovation in your organisation through a tailored approach to the problems you seek to solve.

Program Development

Corporates have a range of tools to accelerate a start-up's growth. Financial support is only one of these tools. These organisations can also support start-ups with customer acquisition and market insights, brand exposure and trust, advisory and mentoring, and providing access to customer touchpoints, mass communication, and payments channels. Startinev will work with you to identify the tools necessary to achieve your organisational goals through collaboration with startups. This can be through development of programs to ehance a capture value from the startup ecosystem.
